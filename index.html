About

Delane Kane is a full-time first year student in Lethbridge Polytechnic's multimedia production course.

His favorite courses within the program so far are graphic design and scripting for multimedia and web since he enjoys both creativity as well as logical thinking. One day he hopes to do animation or game design. These tie into multimedia production through animation, coding, and graphic design. He hopes to take this knowledge and turn his hobbies into a career.

Drawing has always been big influences for Delane. He has been drawing for as long as he can remember and has always wanted to take a more creative path in life. Delane aslo loves technology and what it can do. Be it building computers, coding or using the digital softwares to further enhance his art and design. Multimedia production is perfect because he likes to learn as much new things as possible. It is also great to figure out what the best focus going forward, with how many different pathways there are to try in the program.

Hobbies and Interests

Delane loves to watch anime, play video games, as well as draw. Most of his drawings are based in an anime style. He always has a character in the works. Besides his normal hobbies, Delane also enjoys going out to play sports such as basketball. His favorite sport is badminton. He loves the strategy of where to hit the shuttle as well as the quick precise movements.

Delane always wanted to be an artist when growing up but this dream transitioned more into the digital world as technology advanced more and more, which is what led him to multimedia production.
