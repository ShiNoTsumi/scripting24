Attractions

What to do in Salmon Arm.

Main Attractions

The main attractions for people of all ages that bring in tourists to Salmon Arm are the lakes, cabins, houseboats, and camping locations. These all make people want to take vacations for the summer and visit the lovely beaches and relax or play. The beaches are always bright and bustling during the summer and are perfect for boating, swimming, paddle boarding, or even just relaxing in the sand or grass.

Other Attractions

With the trans Canada going straight through Salmon Arm it also gets traffic from all over Canada, so the sights and all the little stores can attract people on their way to other places. There are also other things to do such as hiking. In the winter you can also enjoy skiing either cross country or at the Larch Hills Ski hill which is right out of Salmon arm.
