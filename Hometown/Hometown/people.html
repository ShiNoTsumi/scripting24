People

Population

Salmon Arm being so beautiful it attracts many looking to retire or start a new life. The population is roughly around 20,000 people. Salmon Arm mainly consists of elderly people, with about 5,000 people over the age of 60 and almost half the population over 50 - years - old. This is due to the scenery, quietness, and activities available. There is still some diversity in age and the schools, and activities for the younger generation are not lacking.

Community

Salmon Arm is quiet and everybody mostly keeps to themselves. Everyone is respectful and are willing to lend a hand if needed. It isn't an extremely closely knit community but that's what makes it great if you want to relax and enjoy the nature that B.C has to offer. This is displayed well in the community slogan for Salmon Arm ”Harmony with Nature.” Everyone enjoys the Nature and what Salmon Arm has to offer and that is mutually shared and enjoyed.
