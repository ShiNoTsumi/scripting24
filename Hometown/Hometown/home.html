What is Salmon Arm?

Are you looking to find a great place to relax and take in the wonders and sights that nature has to offer, then look to Salmon Arm, B.C. The city of Salmon Arm is one of the best places to retire and settle down in. Itâ€™s a quiet, relaxing place to call home or even just take a vacation. Its name is derived from two things, the first is because it is one of the 4 arms of the Shuswap Lake and the second based around the fact that there were many droves of salmon that used to run down the creeks that drain into the lake around Salmon Arm. There are plenty of beautiful sites of nature all around, and many things to do along with that. It feels more like a small tourist town with many small shops where you can find neat little things you wouldn't elsewhere. It is also not as vast or occupied as the other cities around, adding to the feeling of it being a small,quiet town, despite being a city.
