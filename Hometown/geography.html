Geography

location

Salmon Arm is located in British Columbia within the North Okanagan - Shuswap region. It is between many of the bigger cities in B.C such as Kelowna, Vernon, and Kamloops. Within the shuswap itself there are many smaller communities tightly knit together, through the lake and rivers flowing into it. Not only that, Salmon Arm is between Calgary and Vancouver, with the Trans Canada Highway going straight through it making it simple to navigate and locate.

Surrounding Geography

Being in B.C and specifically in the Okanagan, that also means there are tall vast mountains and sprawling lakes everywhere. With Salmon Arm itself being right on the south of the Shuswap Lake which spans approximately 310 square kilometers and has over 400 kilometers of shoreline and is one of the best house boating lakes across all of Canada. The almost never ending forests, expansive winding lakes, and towering snow peaked mountains all make you feel and be connected with nature even within the city itself.
