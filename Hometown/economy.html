Economy

Economic Details

Salmon Arm is the main economic hub of the Shuswap area. There aren't many jobs that separate Salmon Arm from anywhere else but it meets the standards for most any industry. This includes restaurants, stores, schools, and a hospital. Some of the industries with the most influence within Salmon Arm are agriculture and Forestry which have both played a big role for a long time.

Job Availability

There is a plethora of work available as the businesses in Salmon Arm are always looking for new employees. It is a really great place to grow up for the younger generation as there are many part-time jobs available. There are also many different schools, day cares, and a college, adding to the fact that it is a great place for people to settle down and start a life or family.
